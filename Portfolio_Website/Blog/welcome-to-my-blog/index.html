Welcome to My Blog! Sit Down, Relax, and Enjoy Your Stay!

5/1/2024

Well well well... you made it all the way here. Sadly for you (the reader) you are now subject to my inner thoughts, worst ideas, and potentially the biggest letdowns in the history of mankind.

In all seriousness though, I'm happy that you are reading this! Let me tell you a little about why I even have this being done in the first place.

To be frank, I made this website in the hopes of creating something for other people to see. Being one year out of undergrad and soon to start grad school, I realized that I don't have many personal projects to show as a testament to my creativity. That seemed a bit underwhelming in my mind, especially since I have all of these skills that make me perfectly capable of crafting something that I would be proud of.

I also would like the series of blog posts soon to come out to be a documentation of self... this is a unique opportunity to not only let the world know about me, but also to track my personal progress as I grow in all aspects of life.

So here it is! My first portfolio website and a blog to go with it! I'm hoping to do 1-2 of these blog posts a month, just to keep me honest and diligent, and potentially throw some pictures in here to showcase some other personal projects or life events

In the meantime, I hope you've enjoyed getting to know a little bit about me, and be on the lookout for an "actual" blog post coming out in the next few weeks!
